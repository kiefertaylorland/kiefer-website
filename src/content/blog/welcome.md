Welcome to my corner of the internet.

I've been meaning to put together a personal site and blog for a while now. This is it — a place to share thoughts on software testing, cybersecurity, AI/LLM security, music, and whatever else catches my attention.

## What to Expect

I'm a software tester by day, cybersecurity professional by education, and musician by passion. This site is going to reflect all of that:

- **Software testing** — insights from the field, especially around AI/LLM testing
- **Cybersecurity** — thoughts on security in the age of AI
- **Music** — occasional posts about production, DJing, or gear
- **Tech** — whatever I'm building, breaking, or learning

## Why Now

I'm working on some projects that I want to document publicly. Building in public is the best way to stay accountable and connect with people working on similar things.

Stick around. Things are just getting started.
